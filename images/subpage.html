History of the Church

The Bethel Church was formed September 2nd, 1981 with 27 charter members. It was organized by the Green River Association of Free Will Baptist on June 6th, 1982. In the year 1985 the Bethel Church left the Green River Association of Free Will Baptist after a unified vote of all members present. In the year 1985 the Bethel Church Incorporated into a Independent Church, meaning it was no longer a part of the National or any Association. However not to implicate that we do not need or desire fellowship with other churches, we desire and need the fellowship, prayers, help and support of all true believers in our community and afar.
